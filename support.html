Safety Reminder

Stop any exercise that causes sharp pain, dizziness, numbness, or unusual shortness of breath. If you are pregnant, injured, recovering from surgery, or managing a medical condition, speak with a qualified medical professional before training.

Account Deletion And Data Reset

You can delete your Glutefit account directly from the Settings tab inside the app. Open Settings, choose Delete account, and confirm the deletion. This removes your Firebase Authentication sign-in account and clears saved Glutefit app data from that device.

You can also reset your training preferences and workout logs from the Settings tab without deleting your account. Apple subscriptions must still be managed from your Apple ID settings.
