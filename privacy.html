Glutefit Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 7, 2026

Overview

Glutefit is designed as a fitness coaching app with required account sign-up after onboarding. In the current version, workout logs and onboarding preferences are stored locally on your device and are not sent to a Glutefit server.

Account Sign-Up

Glutefit uses Firebase Authentication for email and password account sign-up and login. Firebase may process your email address, account identifier, authentication tokens, and technical information needed to provide secure account access.

Your account is used to protect access to Glutefit and support subscription-related app access. It is not used for third-party advertising tracking.

Information Stored On Your Device

Glutefit may store the following information locally on your device:

  • Training preferences selected during onboarding, such as training location, experience level, goals, days per week, and equipment.
  • Workout logs, including completed workouts, exercise sets, reps, weight, difficulty rating, and notes you enter.

This information is used to personalize your plan, show progress, and help you track training consistency.

Analytics And Advertising Measurement

Glutefit uses Firebase Analytics to understand product usage, such as app opens, onboarding completion, screen views, paywall views, workout completion, purchase events, and restore-purchase events. These analytics help us improve the app experience.

Glutefit may use Meta app events to measure the performance of ads on Facebook, Instagram, and other Meta technologies. Meta may process app event information, device or app information, advertising identifiers when permitted by your device settings and Apple tracking permission, and event metadata such as registration, paywall views, trial starts, subscription purchases, and workout completion.

Glutefit does not send your email address, workout notes, body measurements, or progress photos to Firebase Analytics or Meta app events.

Information We Do Not Collect

In the current version, Glutefit does not collect payment card details, does not collect precise location, and does not sell personal data.

Purchases And Subscriptions

Glutefit uses Apple in-app purchases and RevenueCat to manage subscriptions, purchase validation, entitlements, and restore purchases. Apple handles payment card details. RevenueCat may process subscription status, purchase history, product identifiers, app user identifiers, device or app information, and transaction-related records needed to provide subscription functionality.

Purchase events may also be used for analytics and advertising measurement so we can understand whether ads lead to trial starts or subscriptions.

Support Emails

If you contact Glutefit support by email, your email address and message will be used only to respond to your request. Email communication may be processed by your email provider and our support inbox provider.

Data Controls

You can clear your saved training preferences and workout logs from the Settings tab inside Glutefit by using the reset data option.

Fitness And Safety

Glutefit provides general fitness education and workout organization. It is not medical advice and does not replace guidance from a medical professional, physical therapist, or qualified fitness professional.

Changes To This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as Glutefit changes. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised date.
